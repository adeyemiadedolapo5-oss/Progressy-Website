The Energy Gap

Over 86 million Nigerians lack reliable electricity access. Many rural communities receive less than 30 minutes of electricity per week. This energy gap limits education, safety, and economic growth.

Traditional grid infrastructure and mainstream solar providers rarely reach these underserved areas.

Our Solution

Progressy builds decentralized solar infrastructure for communities without grid access:

Solar Home Systems

Reliable solar power for households and small businesses.

Community Charging Hubs

Shared solar infrastructure for community access.

Technician Training

Local training for youth and women as solar technicians.

Productive-Use Infrastructure

Solar-powered solutions for agriculture and economic growth.
